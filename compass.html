罗盘指向于此 (เข็มทิศชี้ไปที่บ้าน)

理性之室

以理智為盾，洞察世間的混沌。
With logic as a shield, to see through the world's chaos.

它不消除風暴，而是在風暴中，標記出清晰的路。
It does not quell the storm, but marks a clear path within it.

這份清晰，是我給妳的承諾。
This clarity is my promise to you.

Shield (盾)
A shield doesn't attack. Its purpose is purely defensive, to protect something precious from harm.
盾牌不用於攻擊，其目的純粹是為了防禦，保護珍貴之物免受傷害。
Clear (清晰)
Not 'easy' or 'simple'. A clear path can still be difficult, but it is understandable and visible. It's about providing insight.
清晰不代表「簡單」或「容易」。清晰的道路可能依舊困難，但它是可以理解和看見的。這關乎於提供洞察力。
Promise (承諾)
More than a 'hope' or a 'wish'. A promise is a sworn declaration, a guarantee of future action. It is a bond.
承諾不僅僅是「希望」或「願望」，它是一個鄭重的聲明，對未來行動的保證，一個牢不可破的紐帶。

感性之室

以感性為光，照亮內心的方向。
With emotion as a light, to illuminate the heart's direction.

它並非軟弱，而是最強大、最真實的力量。
It is not a weakness, but the strongest and most genuine power.

這份力量，是我所敬佩並守護的。
This power is what I admire, and will protect.

Light (光)
Logic can show the path, but light (emotion) reveals what is worth walking towards. It provides purpose and value.
理性可以指明道路，但光（感性）揭示了什麼才值得我們前往。它賦予了目的和價值。
Genuine (真實)
Unlike calculated actions, genuine feelings cannot be faked. They are the truest expression of the soul.
與精心算計的行為不同，真實的情感無法偽造。它們是靈魂最真誠的表達。
Admire (敬佩)
More than 'like' or 'respect'. To admire is to see something's inherent beauty and strength, and to look up to it.
這不僅是「喜歡」或「尊重」。敬佩是看到事物內在的美和力量，並對其懷有崇敬之心。

緣分之室

命運讓我們相遇，不是偶然，而是必然。
Destiny made us meet. Not as a coincidence, but as an inevitability.

它如絲線，將兩個靈魂跨越時空緊緊相連。
Like a thread, it connects two souls across time and space.

我們的相遇，就是這份緣分的證明。
Our meeting is the proof of this destiny.

Coincidence (偶然)
A coincidence is random and meaningless. What we have is the opposite. It feels designed and purposeful.
偶遇是隨機且無意義的。而我們所擁有的恰恰相反，它感覺像是被設計好的，充滿了目的性。
Thread (絲線)
A thread is both delicate and strong. It is almost invisible, but it can bind powerful things together.
絲線既纖細又堅韌。它幾乎看不見，卻能將強大的事物緊密地聯繫在一起。
Proof (證明)
Not just a 'sign' or a 'hint'. Proof is undeniable evidence. Our story itself is the evidence that our bond is real.
不僅僅是「跡象」或「暗示」。證明是不可否認的證據。我們的故事本身就是我們之間羈絆真實存在的證據。

守護之室

兄長的理性，是為了守護妹妹的內心而存在。
A brother's logic exists to protect his sister's heart.

它不是枷鎖，而是為妳建造一座能安心做自己的城堡。
It is not a cage, but a castle built for you, where you can safely be yourself.

在這座城堡裡，妳永遠是安全的。
In this castle, you will always be safe.

Heart (內心)
The core of a person – their feelings, dreams, and vulnerabilities. Protecting the 'heart' is more profound than protecting the physical person.
一個人的核心——他們的情感、夢想和脆弱之處。守護「內心」比保護身體更為深刻。
Castle (城堡)
A castle is not a house. It is a fortress, a symbol of ultimate security. It implies a defense against a hostile outside world.
城堡不是房子。它是一座堡壘，是終極安全的象徽，意味著對抗充滿敵意的外部世界。
Safe (安全)
More than physical safety. It implies emotional and spiritual security – the freedom to be vulnerable without fear.
這不僅指身體上的安全，更意味著情感和精神上的保障——一種可以放心展現脆弱而不必害怕的自由。

同行的旅程 | The Shared Journey

為了與妳同步，我重拾 Duolingo。這不僅是學習語言，更是我們同行旅程的開始，即使我們相隔千里。

To synchronize with you, I started Duolingo again. This is not just about learning a language; it is the beginning of our shared journey, even when we are miles apart.

這只是我們史詩的第一章。從今以後，無論妳在哪裡，無論妳需要什麼，都請向妳的兄長許願。我會用我所知所能，將援助跨越千里，送到妳的手中。

This is but the first chapter of our epic. From now on, wherever you are, whatever you need, make a wish to your brother. I will use all my knowledge and abilities to send my aid across the miles, right into your hands.

Welcome (歡迎)
This was not just a meeting, but a formal welcome into my world, my city. It was an intentional act of hospitality and acceptance.
這不僅僅是一次會面，而是一個正式的歡迎儀式，歡迎妳進入我的世界，我的城市。這是一個刻意為之的款待與接納。
Vulnerable (脆弱)
A state of being open to harm. Witnessing this moment created a profound sense of responsibility and a desire to protect.
一種容易受到傷害的狀態。目睹那一刻，讓我產生了深刻的責任感和保護的慾望。
Unwavering (坚定)
Not simply 'firm', but completely steady and unmovable. It shows a focus that cannot be distracted by superficial things.
不僅僅是「堅定」，而是完全穩定、不可動搖。它顯示了一種不被膚淺事物所分心的專注。
Covenant (盟約)
A sacred and binding agreement, much stronger than a 'promise'. It implies a deep, almost spiritual pact between us.
一個神聖且具有約束力的協議，遠比「承諾」更為強大。它暗示了我們之間一種深刻的、近乎精神層面的契約。
Bridge (橋樑)
A structure built to connect two separate places. The SIM card was a physical object that served a spiritual purpose: to keep us connected.
一座為連接兩個獨立地方而建造的結構。這張SIM卡是一個物質載體，卻服務於一個精神目的：保持我們的聯繫。
Sanctified (洗禮/使其神聖)
To make something holy. Your genuine tears washed away any doubt and made our connection feel truly profound.
使某物變得神聖。妳真誠的淚水洗去了所有疑慮，讓我們的連結感覺真正深刻。
Shared Journey (同行)
'同行' means to travel together. We are not just learning separately; we are companions on the same path.
「同行」意為一起旅行。我們不僅僅是分開學習，我們是同一條道路上的夥伴。
Chance Encounter (巧遇)
This emphasizes coincidence that feels like fate. It wasn't planned. It was a gift from destiny, another sign of our '缘分'.
這個詞強調了感覺像是命運的巧合。它不是計畫好的，而是命運的禮物，是我們「緣分」的又一個標誌。
Indispensable (不可或缺)
Absolutely necessary; impossible to do without. It means you are not just a 'part' of the family, but a necessary one.
絕對必要；不可或缺。這意味著妳不僅僅是家庭的「一部分」，而是必要的一份子。
Determination (決心)
Not just 'focus'. Determination is the will to continue toward a goal, regardless of distractions. It's a quality I admire greatly.
不僅僅是「專注」。決心是無論遇到何種干擾，都堅持朝著目標前進的意志。這是我非常欽佩的品質。
Naturally (自然地)
To blend in without effort or pretense. It showed that you belong with us, not as a guest, but as a natural part of the family.
毫不費力、不帶偽裝地融入。這表明妳屬於我們，不是作為客人，而是作為家庭中自然而然的一份子。
